Combat In The Nether

The Nether is a hellish dimension which you can access by creating a nether portal in the Overworld. It's worth the trip, because you can find many useful items here that aren't available anywhere else.
You'll need a minimum of 10 blocks of obsidian to build a Nether Portal. Once you've constructed the portal, use a flint and steel or a fire charge to activate it, and jump through.




Nether Portal

A full Nether Portal can be crafted from 14 blocks of obsidian. If you're short on obsidian, save yourself 4 blocks, and cut out the corners, it'll still work!
